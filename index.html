Architecture

From enterprise systems to governed AI action.

RACC acts as the governed control plane between enterprise systems, reusable capabilities and multiple AI runtimes.

Capability Catalog

Reusable governed actions, knowledge and tool definitions with clear ownership and lifecycle.

Policy & Permissions

Identity-aware rules, approvals, guardrails, audit scope and execution constraints.

Routing & Orchestration

Selects the right capability, system connector, AI runtime and execution path for each task.

Audit & Observability

Full traceability for requests, model use, data sources, policy decisions and outcomes.

MCP Adoption Factory

Adopt existing MCP servers or create new ones, wrap them as governed capability packs and publish them safely.

Runtime Choice

Local AI, cloud AI, customer AI, private AI or deterministic execution based on cost, risk and policy.
